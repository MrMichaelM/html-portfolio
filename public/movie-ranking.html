The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Great Escape

This is my favorite heist type movie.
Great cast and a fun escape plot!

Spritied Away

Wonderful coming of age story with spectacular animation!

Wall-E

The first thirty minutes of the film showcase the power of expression over words.
Plus, it's the first place I heard La Vie en Rose, the song my wife I danced did our first dance too.
